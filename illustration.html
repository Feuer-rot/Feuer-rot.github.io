Feste Feiern

An Illustration for a wallpaper in the exhibition "Feste feiern" at MK&G Hamburg.

As a group, we reimagined the Panathenaic procession as depicted on the Parthenon frieze. Each illustrating a section of the procession

Abriss

An fictional card game in which players attempt to rescue iconic buildings of Hamburg — either already demolished or threatened with demolition

Hand-painted in watercolors.

Schwimmen

The classic card game Schwimmen, traditionally played with a standard deck, reimagined with an environmental twist.
In this version, each player takes on the role of a sea creature and receives a personal play deck to track their remaining lives.

But beware — instead of just losing points, you risk drowning in ocean pollution and trash.

Lithography

Lithographs created during a seminar at the Lithographic Academy in Tidaholm, Sweden.

ABOUT ME

Hi! I’m Mailin, an Illustration Student at HAW in Hamburg, a creative developer and digital artist with a love for all things visual and interactive.
I enjoy working where creativity meets technology— whether it's crafting 3D models, building smooth web interfaces, or bringing ideas to life through animation. I want to create things that invite curiosity — playful, visual, and a bit offbeat in all the right ways.

When I’m not in front of a screen, you’ll probably find me sketching, exploring new creative tools, or out on an outdoor adventure.
